Why does Innovate UK ask for personal data?

By answering these questions you’re helping us to ensure Innovate UK’s as inclusive as possible and treating everyone fairly. It should take you no more than 10 minutes.

By embedding equality, diversity and inclusion in everything we do, we maximise the impact of our funding. The benefits of this are that:

  • Everything we fund embraces a diversity of thought, people and ideas
  • A wide range of innovators have access to the Innovate UK resources, advice and opportunities they need to succeed
  • All parts of society benefit.

The information you give us will be part of your Innovation Funding Service (IFS) profile. You can edit it at any time.

How Innovate UK will use your personal data

We use the personal data you give us to:

  • Identify any under-represented groups, investigate the reasons for this and take action to improve representation
  • Publish aggregated and anonymised data as part of our commitment to transparency and supporting access and inclusion. For example, application and success rates.

We’ll store your data securely and handle it in line with UK data protection legislation.

If you would like to know more, including how to exercise your data protection rights, please see our privacy notice.
